The starting point

Sportmix was a startup aiming to help sport amateurs find co-players, sport events and locations from their city. Our vision was to create not only a platform, but a community bringing people together through casual sport.

Role: Founding member, Product Designer

Product highlights

Allows users to find co-players and helps people to find sport locations & events in their current city. Builds a community around sport lovers and helps them discover new interesting sports

Challenge

Currently people are starting to be more aware about the importance of their health and there is a growing trend of going to the gym. Activities by interacting with friends or other people are a healthier and more enjoyable way to spend the time destined to exercise. Having this in mind, we noticed a really common problem when people what to practice a team sport - not having available friends or enough team members to properly do it.

Goals

Help people find co-players every time they plan to do sport, connectiong them in the real world using a virtual incentive, making practicing team sports more accessible.

Solution

From a list of features, we narrowed it down to the core 4 that would make up our MVP.

Feature 1: Discover sport locations from the current city

WHAT: Users are provided with a database of sport locations from their city.

WHY: Helps people to discover where they can practice their desired sport.

GOAL: Users can get started more quickly discovering sport locations and partner locations increase their visibility and bookings.

Feature 2: Find sport events near and join them

WHAT: Users are provided with a list of sport events in locations near them.

WHY: Helps users find sport events to join.

GOAL: Encourage players to constantly check upcoming events and join them.

Feature 3: Create a sport event

WHAT: Allows users to create their own sport event and gather co-players.

Taking the brand further

Sportmix brand is based on 4 major values: freedom, healthiness, community and fun.

The name

The name concept is based on 2 words- sport & mix. This has a 2 main interpretations:

- The mix of sport categories that you can experience on our platform

- The mix of people that are building the community

Another criteria for choosing this name was the melody and the easy to remember quality so that can be appealing to a larger spectrum of users.
