The starting point

Sportmix was a startup aiming to help sport amateurs find co-players, sport events and locations from their city. Our vision was to create not only a platform, but a community bringing people together through casual sport. A meet-up for sport lovers.

Role: Founding member, Product Designer

Product highlights

Helps people find players, sport locations & events in their current city. Builds a community around sport lovers.

Challenge

Currently people are starting to be more aware about the importance of their health and there is a growing trend of going to the gym. Activities by interacting with friends or other people are a healthier and more enjoyable way to spend the time destined to exercise. Having this in mind, we noticed a really common problem when people what to practice a team sport - not having available friends or enough team members to properly do it.

Goals

Help people find co-players every time they plan to do sport, connectiong them in the real world using a virtual incentive, making practicing team sports more accessible.

Solution

Our soultion was to create a web platform with the posibility to extend as an mobile app once it experienced traction. From a list of features, we narrowed it down to the core 4 that would make up our MVP.

Feature 1: Search sport locations/events/users from a city

WHAT: Users are provided with a database of sport locations/events/users from their city.

WHY: Helps people to discover where they can practice their desired sport, find events and other people with who to play.

GOAL: Users can get started more quickly discovering events in sport locations and partner locations increase their visibility and bookings.

Feature 2: Join a sport event

WHAT: Users are provided with more informations about the selected event and with the ability to join the event.

WHY: Helps people to organise the event and not overbook the spots.

GOAL: Ecourage users to join events.

Feature 3: Create a sport event

WHAT: Users are able to not only join existing events, but create their own using the available locations database.

WHY: Allows users to create their own sport event and find players.

GOAL: Encourage users to book locations and organise sport meetups.

Feature 4: Give feedback to users

WHAT: Request users to give a "one click" feedback to their co-players.

WHY: Allows users to filter out bad people.

GOAL: Keep the comunity as harmonious as possible.

Use cases

01. Search for events for a specific sport

- Log in and browse existing events for a particular sport

- Find event and join it

- After the end date of the event, give/get user feedback

02. Search for events at a particular location

- Log in and search for a specific sport location

- Check active events at that location and join them

- After the end date of the event, give/get user feedback

03. Create an event if there are none existing

- Log in and search events for a particular sport/location

- If no events available, create event

- After the end date of the event, give/get user feedback

04. Explore randomly for sports, events or users

- Log in and explore random events/locations or users

- Join event

- After the end date of the event, give/get user feedback

Taking the brand further

Sportmix brand is based on 4 major values: freedom, healthiness, community and fun.

The concept for the name is based on two words - sport & mix that could be interpreted as the mix of sport categories that you can experience on the platform or the mix of people that are building the community. Another criteria for choosing this name was the melody and the easy to remember quality so that can be appealing to a larger spectrum of users.
