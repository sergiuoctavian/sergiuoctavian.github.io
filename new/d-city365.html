The starting point

City365 was a startup aiming to help bars/restaurants & other venues to bring customers at their locations during the dead hours by providing a limited time deals system.

Product highlights

- Saves pocket money for students by providing good deals

- Brings more people to venues creating a constant flow of customers during all open ours usin AI integrated with the POS System

- Helps the consumers find new locations and the business owners to market themselves better and increse profit

Challenge

During the open hours, there are time frames when venues are overcrowded with customers and periods when there are the so called "dead hours". The challenge was to regulate the flow of customers by atracting students using limited time offers durind the time when the location is empty.

Goals

Create a win-win system that will help business owners have more control over the flow of customers, increasing profit and also helping sudents have fun and save pocket money.







Solution

The MVP is based on 3 main features as a core experience.The MVP is based on 3 main features as a core experience.The MVP is based on 3 main features as a core experience.The MVP is based on 3 main features as a core experience.

Feature 1: Location Discovery

WHAT: Users are provided with a list of locations sorted by proximity

WHY: Helps users find new locations or provide loyal customers a way to select their favourite location in order to see what offers are active.

GOAL: Loyal customers can get the offer from their favourite place quickly. Regular users can discover interesting locations. Business owners have more visibility.

Feature 2: Offers Discovery

WHAT: Users are provided with a feed of Offers and informations about active time and distance (location)

WHY: Helps users pick offer varieties that fits best with their desires, making it more likely to lead them successfuly to the venue.

GOAL: Encourage users to go to the venue by providing atractive offers, which helps the business owners increase their customer base and profit.

Feature 3: Offer Details & Scan

WHAT: Users are provided with more offer details.

WHY: Users can see more details regarding the offer they consider to choose before actually taking it.

GOAL: Encourage users to take the offer stimulating them visualy and minimizing the unused deals error margins.
