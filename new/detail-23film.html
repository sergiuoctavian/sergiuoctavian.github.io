23FiLM

Website Design & Development

A touch to the Film Industry

Was a pleasure to develop the web presence for 23FiLM. An independent Film Production Company aiming to produce Movies, Commercials, Corporate Videos and New-Media content using the newest equipment on the market and a passionate team of movie makers.

Year 2016

Visit Site

_

The client needed

1. online visibility for showcasing both client and inhouse projects
2. brand adjustment in order to better project their personality and unlock their target audience

_

The strategy

The brand had a minimalistic approach and desired a similar direction for their website. The challenge was to create a fresh web presence, complementing and improoving the existing brand by focusing on their personality in order to stand out and atract customers from their medium to high target audience.

_

The approach

Vibråntmatter designed and developed every piece of online visual communication (from website to marketing collaterals such as: coming soon landing page, facebook cover & profile picture), proposing different solutions to promote online.

_

Color Palette

The concept uses subtile yet empowering color accents as key elements, in addition to the the “camera obscura” inspired black and white - light and shadow theme, aiming to give a memorable experience to the user, transcending him into the ambiance. Every color si asociated to a main service category (corporate, commercials, documentary, film) based on memory asociative laws.



_

Typography

The use of bold oversized titles gives the brand a feel of legitimity and an healthy amount of boldness in aproaching their projects combined with a desire to make a statement. The strong typography is one of the main elements in comunicating their personality. The main source of inspiration was their logo that already projected a particular feel that needed to be present in the design for keeping the consistance.

Typeface: Helvetica Neue
