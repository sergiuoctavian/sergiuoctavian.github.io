Website Design & Development

A digital catwalk

Was a pleasure to develop the web presence for Arabela, a fashion designer from Milan. Her brand, Arabela Sim has a minimalistic approach to design and desired an outstanding and similar direction transposed into digital.

Year 2016

Visit Site

_

The client needed

1. online visibility for showcasing collections
2. a website concept that is clean, yet intaractive and personal

_

The strategy

The brand had a minimalistic approach, the challenge being to keep the same direction for their website, creating a concept that is complementing her design style and emphaseing on the images.

_

The approach

Vibråntmatter designed and developed every piece of online visual communication (from website to marketing collaterals such as: coming soon landing page, facebook cover & profile picture), proposing different solutions to promote online.

_

Color Palette

The concept uses different monocrome shades, aiming for facilitate the images to be the main visua comunication elements.



_

Typography

The use of oversized light titles gives a touch of softness to the concept. Her logo was already using light lines and projected a particular feel that is present in the layout building a stronger brand using the consistance.
