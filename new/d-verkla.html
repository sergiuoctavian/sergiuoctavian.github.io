The starting point

It is always a pleasure to collaborate with Jonathan on projects that have a positive impact on environmental sustainability. Verkla is a company offering research & co-design services for clients situated into the eco market. The goal of Verkla is to help make green projects successful, through great research and design. The task was to create a digital face for the brand, a one page ( love ) showcasing their services and design approach in order to attract more customers from the green sector.







Taking the brand further

The concept is based on two different style directions. One is more bold and energic using colors that illustrates the temperature, suggesting the climate change. The other one, is more tone down, focusing on green stuff.
