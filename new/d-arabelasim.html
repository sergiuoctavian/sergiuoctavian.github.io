The starting point

Arabela is a fashion designer from Milan that desired online visbility for showcasing her new brand. Arabela Sim - has a minimalistic approach to fashion, using as a primary style element the white fabric blended with overlaping textures as signature details. The challenge was to keep the same direction for her web presence, creating a concept that is complementing her design style and helps building a stronger brand.







Taking the brand further

The concept is based on different monochrome shades, aiming for utilising the images as the main visual communication element. The use of oversized light weight and fixed position titles gives a touch of softness to the concept and also somehow mimics the overlapping texture details from her clothing articles. The character of her logo is based on light lines creating a soft letter shape that is projecting a particular feel wich was the inspiration for the layout, in order to add more consistency to her brand over different mediums.
