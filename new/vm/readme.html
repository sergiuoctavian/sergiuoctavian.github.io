profile —

My name is Sergiu-Octavian. I'm an independent designer with an excitement for coding. I'm focusing on building high-end, nonconformist yet usable digital designs for bold customers like you.

I have a batchelor degree in fine arts, specialised in design. Despite that, I taught myself front end development during my youth because I like to get involved into the building process of digital products and experiences that I design and in general I like to make stuff. I don't limit myself just to that, being excited about everything tech. Monotony is evil, that's why from time to time, print projects are the missing key to my creative flow so I gladly take from time to time print projects.

I have been designing for web&print for over 4 years within start-ups, companies or with individuals that are into creative, cultural or environmental fields and I am always up to approach a new challenge.

approach —

I like to experiment a lot and I always strive to keep an open mind and constantly learn. Usually I manipulate shades of black and white, using color accents only to highlight important elements or for branding purposes. I believe simple is not only functional, but beautiful. In the same time, I love the really clever small details that make the difference.

philosophy —

why vibrant matter?

Everything looks still into our eyes, but the world is vibrating. All material things are vibrating at us in this moment and we are vibrating back at them. The micro and macro cosmos are actually the same and we are all part of it.

The creative process can be defined as the action of deconstructing something in order to reconstruct it in a different way. In this process, new vibratons are created and once we recognize that all matter is actually vibrating energy, we can begin to form a new vision for the world, ethics and interaction.
