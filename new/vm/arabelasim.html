Arabela Sim —

A minimal fashion digital showroom for Arabela Sim, inspired by her approach of using in her pices as primary element the white fabric, overlapped by textures as a signature style.

— 2016

the concept —

The concept for the website is based on different monochrome shades, aiming for utilising the images as the main visual communication element.

The use of oversized light weight and fixed position titles gives a touch of softness to the website and also somehow mimics the overlapping texture details from her clothing collections.



The character of her logo is based on light lines creating a soft letter shape that is projecting a particular feel wich was the inspiration for the layout aiming to add more consistency to her brand over different mediums.
