city365 —

City365 was a startup aiming to help bars/restaurants & other venues to bring customers at their locations during the dead hours by providing a limited time deals system.

— 2014

Product highlights

The product is based on a win-win approach, helping students to save pocket money by providing them with good deals at certain locations. This brings more people to venues, creating a constant and controlled flow of customers during all open hours by using AI integrated POS Systems with the goal of incresing the profit.




Challenges

First, students have a very well developed social behavior but not always enough money for it. Secondly, during the open hours, there are time frames when venues are overcrowded with customers and the so-called "dead hours" periods. The challenge was to regulate the flow of customers by attracting students using limited time offers during the time when the location is empty.

Goals

The goal was to help business owners have more control over the flow of customers, increasing their profit by helping students enjoy their social life on a budget.

Solution

The solution was in the form of a mobile app that will display the limited time offers to the students automatically based on the free tables available in the venue. The MVP is based on 3 main features as a core experience.

Feature 1

Offers Discovery

WHAT: Users are provided with a feed of Offers and information about active time and distance from them.

WHY: Helps users pick from a variety of offers the one that fits the best with their desire, making it more likely to lead them successfully to a venue.

GOAL: Encourage users to go to the venue by providing attractive offers, which helps the business owners increase their customer base and profit.

Feature 2

Location Discovery

WHAT: Users are provided with a list of locations sorted by proximity

WHY: Helps users find new locations or provide to loyal customers a way to select their favorite location in order to check what offers are active there.

GOAL: Facilitate to loyal customers a quick way to get an offer from their favorite place and keep them loyal. Regular users can discover interesting new locations. New locations get more visibility.

Feature 3

Offer Details & Scan

WHAT: Users are provided with more visual details about the offer in the shape of a virtual discount card.

WHY: Users can see more details regarding the offer they consider to choose before actually taking it.

GOAL: Encourage users to take the offer stimulating them visually and minimizing the unused deals error margins.

Taking the brand further

The concept is based on 4 main attributes: friendly, exciting, youthful, cheap. Psychology of color consists of neutral tones with strong exciting highlights inspired by the Coca-Cola/McDonald's proven model as a subliminal incentive for the users to want to take action and get the offers. The philosophy behind the predominant round and bulky UI style is to give the app a very approachable and friendly feeling.
