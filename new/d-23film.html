The starting point

Was a pleasure to develop the web presence for 23FiLM. An independent Film Production Company aiming to produce Movies, Commercials, Corporate Videos and New-Media content using the newest equipment on the market and a passionate team of movie makers.

Taking the brand further

The brand had a minimalistic approach and desired a similar direction for their website. The challenge was to create a fresh web presence, complementing and improoving the existing brand by focusing on their personality in order to stand out and atract customers from their medium to high target audience. Vibråntmatter designed and developed every piece of online visual communication (from website to marketing collaterals such as: coming soon landing page, facebook cover & profile picture), proposing different solutions to promote online.
