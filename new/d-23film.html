The starting point

Was a pleasure to develop the web presence for 23FiLM. An independent Film Production Company aiming to produce Movies, Commercials, Corporate Videos and New-Media content using the newest equipment on the market and a passionate team of movie makers. 23FiLM needed online visibility for showcasing both client and inhouse projects and brand adjustment in order to better project their personality and unlock their target audience.







Taking the brand further

The brand had a minimalistic approach and desired a similar direction for their website. The challenge was to create a fresh web presence, complementing and improoving the existing brand by focusing on their personality in order to stand out and atract customers from their medium to high target audience.
Vibråntmatter designed and developed every piece of online visual communication (from website to marketing collaterals such as: coming soon landing page, facebook cover & profile picture), proposing different solutions to promote online. The concept uses subtile yet empowering color accents as key elements, in addition to the the “camera obscura” inspired black and white - light and shadow theme, aiming to give a memorable experience to the user, transcending him into the ambiance. Every color si asociated to a main service category (corporate, commercials, documentary, film) based on memory asociative laws. The use of bold oversized titles gives the brand a feel of legitimity and an healthy amount of boldness in aproaching their projects combined with a desire to make a statement. The strong typography is one of the main elements in comunicating their personality. The main source of inspiration was their logo that already projected a particular feel that needed to be present in the design for keeping the consistance.
